Add a new collection

Check connectivity

To create a new collection, you need to have an active internet connection to be able to retrieve all the necessary data from the database.




Adding a new collection
On home page

You may click on "Add collection" button to create a new collection for the subagardens of your garden in Home page.

On Observations page

You may click on "Add collection" button to create a new collection for the subagardens of your garden in Observations page.

Recording data

After arriving at the "Add collection" page, please, choose the date of the collection (today will be chosen by default), subgarden you would like to record data for, and the plant you would like to work with.

Choosing date

Date of collection will be chosen by default as the current day of collection. However, you may change the date if you wish by clicking on the calendar icon .

Choosing subgarden(s)

After confirming the date of collection, please, choose the subgarden(s) you would like to work in. Subgardens with a preceeding plus sign "+" are not chosen yet. After choosing a garden with a plus sign, it will be available offline and the plus sign will be removed (as seen in the image above).

Choosing a plant

After confirming the collection details, you may start recording data. To do so, you need to choose the plant you would like to record. Plants with a check sign "âœ“" indicate the data for plant was recorded and saved on device successfully.

Ordering plants

Plants are by default ordered by their orders in the given subgarden. However, if you wish to do so, you may sort the plants in alphabetical order too by clicking on the "Sort plants" button.
