Users may enter the values by selecting their responses from the dropdown menus with the help of a mouse. However, for a faster data entering process you may also try keyboard shortcuts. With the help of Tab key you may easily select the next field and with the Tab + CTRL key combination you may select the previous field.

By entering the first letter of the response you can also easily choose the value from a dropdown list. For the flowering intensity and senescence intensity values you only need to type in the first digit or the whole number.
