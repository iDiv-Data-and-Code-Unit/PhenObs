In the Overview view, users are greeted with garden selection menu. Depending on their list of accessible gardens and subgardens, they are provided with available choices.

A user may choose a garden, which are indicated with a bold text font, to retrieve all the collections from the garden’s all subgardens. Or they may also choose a subgarden instead to only retrieve collections for a specific subgarden.

Admins will also have the option to select “All” which will return all the collections in the system from every subgarden.
