After you have finished recording data for your collection, you need to save it to the database. On the “Add collection” and “Edit collection” pages, you can save the collection to the database by clicking on the “Done” button at the bottom right corner of the form. The button will turn blue and can be clicked after you have recorded data for every plant in the collection. Clicking on the button, you will save the collection to the database and you will see a window appearing to confirm your collection was successfully saved.

You can also save the collections in the Local Observations view by clicking on the Save button for the given collection.
