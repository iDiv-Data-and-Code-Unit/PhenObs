You will find all the related fields to be filled in the “Add collection” and “Edit collection” pages. You need to choose the response from the dropdown menus.

In the maintenance section you will find multi select field. You may choose multiple choices here. In the “Remarks” field, you may leave your comments/remarks regarding the collection.

If the observation is not possible for a reason, you may check “No observation possible” at the end of the form. If you have checked this option, then you are required to fill ”Remarks” field.

Previous collection values

On the right side of the fields you may notice a set of buttons. These are the values from the previous collection. At the top, you will see the date to know exactly when that collection was created. You may also edit the values from the previous collection by selecting the field by clicking on it. In the opened window, choose the new value and click on save. Be aware that, you need to save the edited previous collection separately to update the values in the database. To do so, you need to save it in the Local Observations page.

To speed up collection process you may also copy the values from previous collection by clicking on the “Copy from previous collection” button. Be aware that it will not copy maintenance options and remarks.
