After editing a collection on the Overview view, you may save the collection by clicking on the “Save” button on the bottom right corner.

Similarly, you may also edit multiple collections at once and save them all together. To do so, check the checkboxes of the collections you have edited and click on the “Save all” button in the navigation bar.
