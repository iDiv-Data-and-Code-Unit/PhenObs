Deleting a collection

You may need to delete locally stored collections on the device to get rid of clutter. Please, be aware that deleting a collection will only delete the locally stored collection from the device. To remove the collections permanently, contact the authorized staff members as it requires administrator permissions.

To delete the locally stored collections from device, please follow the below steps:

  1. Proceed to Local Observations page.
  2. Locate the collection you would like to delete.
  3. Click on the trashcan icon .
  4. Confirm on the opened dialogue box by clicking “Yes”.
